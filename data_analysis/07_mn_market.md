## MN PBM Transparency Report Alignment

The MN PBM Transparency Report identified these as top drug spend categories. Our PA data confirms:

| MN Report Category | Drugs | MN PA Rate | National PA Rate | Alignment |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Immunological Agents** | 278 | **92%** | 88% | Confirmed — near-universal PA |
| **Antineoplastics** | 262 | **91%** | 89% | Confirmed — near-universal PA |
| **Blood Glucose Regulators** | 173 | 22% | 22% | Mixed — GLP-1s are 100% PA but older diabetes drugs are not |

**Anti-obesity Agents (GLP-1s)** were not separately categorized in our ATC mapping, but semaglutide/tirzepatide/liraglutide all show 100% PA rates nationally and in MN.

The MN PBM report's top spend categories align perfectly with the highest PA burden categories. **The drugs that cost the most are also the ones most gated behind PA** — validating the MN market opportunity.
