# FirstPass Rx Executive Summary

## Specialty Drug Prior Authorization Done Right

---

### The Problem

**Prior authorizations for specialty drugs fail 30-40% of the time—not because patients don't qualify, but because submissions are incomplete.**

Clinics and hospitals submit PAs with outdated labs, missing documentation, and insufficient step therapy evidence. Each rejection means:

- **2+ weeks** of delayed treatment for patients
- **$200+** in administrative rework per denial
- **24%** of patients abandon treatment after PA delays

For high-cost drugs like Mounjaro, Keytruda, and Humira, the stakes are even higher.

---

### The Solution

**FirstPass Rx validates PA submissions against PBM-specific criteria before they're submitted—maximizing first-pass approval rates.**

| What We Do | How It Works |
|------------|--------------|
| **Validate completeness** | Check every required field, document, and data point |
| **Apply PBM-specific rules** | Know what Prime, Express Scripts, CVS Caremark actually need |
| **Flag gaps before submission** | Catch the 91-day-old A1C before the PBM rejects it |
| **Expert review** | Trained specialists QA every submission |

**Target: 85%+ first-pass approval rate** (vs. industry average of 60-65%)
