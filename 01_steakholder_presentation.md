# The Problem

## Prior Authorization is Broken

Every day, patients wait for life-changing medications while paperwork sits in limbo.

---

## The Numbers Tell the Story

| Metric | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **$75.5 billion** | in annual Part D drug spend gated behind PA requirements |
| **28.1%** | of all drug-plan combinations require prior authorization |

| **24%** | of patients abandon treatment after PA delays or denials |

> *Sources: CMS Formulary PUF (Jan 2026), KFF/CMS 2024, AMA Prior Authorization Survey*

---

## For Specialty Drugs, It's Even Worse

Specialty medications—oncology drugs, biologics, GLP-1s—face the highest scrutiny:

- **Higher denial rates** due to complex documentation requirements
- **Stricter step therapy** rules that vary by PBM
- **Costly delays** for time-sensitive treatments
- **Inconsistent criteria** across payers

**A single Mounjaro PA denial can delay treatment by 2+ weeks and cost a practice $200+ in administrative rework.**

---

## The Root Cause

### Submissions fail because they're incomplete—not because patients don't qualify

| What PBMs Need | What Practices Submit |
|----------------|----------------------|
| A1C within 90 days | A1C from 4 months ago |
| Documented step therapy with dates, doses, and failure reasons | "Patient tried metformin" |
| Specific ICD-10 codes | Unspecified diagnosis codes |
| Complete clinical documentation | Partial chart notes |

**The information exists. It's just not making it into the submission.**

# How It Works

---

## The FirstPass Rx Process

### Step 1: Intake

Practice sends us the PA case via secure portal, fax, or EHR integration.

### Step 2: Validation

Our system checks the submission against PBM-specific criteria:

- Required fields complete?
- Labs recent enough?
- Step therapy documented properly?
- Diagnosis codes supported?

### Step 3: Expert Review

A trained PA specialist reviews the case, flags any gaps, and requests additional information if needed.

### Step 4: Clean Submission

Once validated, we submit to the PBM through the optimal channel (ePA, portal, fax).

### Step 5: Tracking & Follow-Up

We track the submission to approval, handle additional info requests, and support appeals if needed.
